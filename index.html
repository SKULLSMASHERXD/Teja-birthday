My Twin

There are some people who don’t just come into your life; they stay, quietly, steadily, in ways that change you forever. You’re that person for me.

From random gubbois and headpats that somehow fix everything, to the way you care without ever making it loud, you’ve always had this rare softness. The kind that doesn’t try to impress, but heals anyway. You’ve been comfort in human form.

I still remember 23rd December, 2025, 8:57 pm. You didn’t need to say much. You just hugged me when I was low, and in that moment, everything felt lighter. That hug meant more than you’ll ever know. It wasn’t just a hug, it was reassurance, safety, and understanding all at once.

You’re the first and only person who has seen almost every version of me — the calm one, the chaotic one, the overthinking one, the childish one, the annoying one. And somehow, you never walked away from any of them. You stayed. Always.

I overthink a lot. I get insecure sometimes. I even get jealous when I feel like I’m not special anymore. But what amazes me is how you never got tired of reassuring me. Never irritated. Never dismissive. You cared patiently, gently, every single time.

I’ve hurt you unintentionally more times than I’d like to admit. Not because I didn’t care — but because I didn’t always know how to handle my emotions. And yet, you stayed. That loyalty, that kindness, that emotional strength, it means the world to me.

I love being childish with you. I love annoying you on purpose just to hear your reactions. I love sharing my most random, senseless rants with you because I know you’ll listen, even when they make no sense at all. With you, I never feel the need to pretend.

You have this beautiful way of making people feel seen. Heard. Valued. And I hope you know how incredibly special that is. How special you are.

Just platonically, but honestly, I love you more than myself. Not in a way that asks for anything back. Just in a way that’s grateful you exist in my life.

I don’t ever want to lose you. Not because I need you to survive, but because life is simply better with you in it.

Thank you for being my twin.

Thank you for being my safe space.

Thank you for being you.

Happy Birthday, Teja. 💙✨
